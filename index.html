FAQ

Questions about
the launch?

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Canopy will connect to Zoho CRM via your API key and to Google via secure authentication. We will never store your credentials. Your data will be encrypted in transit and never hosted on our servers. We're building GDPR compliant from day one, with infrastructure in Europe.

What happens if I cancel?

Your Google Sheet will stay in your Drive — it's yours. Automatic sync simply stops. No hidden fees, no penalties. You'll be able to restart your subscription anytime.

Why Canopy instead of Zapier?

Generic tools like Zapier require complex workflow setup, manual formatting, and constant maintenance. Canopy is purpose-built for Zoho CRM → Google Sheets: pre-built templates, automatic formatting, and a 5-minute setup. No technical knowledge required.

When will Canopy launch?

We're targeting summer 2026. Sign up now to get early access and be among the first users. We'll keep you updated on progress.

What's the difference between Explorer, Pro, and Apex?

Explorer is free forever — 1 standard template, 1,000 rows, daily sync, self-serve support. Pro (€8.99/mo) gives you 3 standard templates, 5,000 rows, sync every 12h plus 3 manual syncs, and 48h email support. Apex (€17.99/mo) unlocks everything: unlimited templates, hourly sync plus on-demand refresh, unlimited rows, dedicated onboarding, and 24h priority support. CRM integrators can also pick the Agency Pack at €39/mo to connect up to 10 client CRMs. All plans cancel anytime, no commitment.

Which Zoho CRM plans are compatible?

Canopy will work with any Zoho CRM plan that has API access — Free, Standard, Professional, and Premium. If you can access your data via the Zoho CRM API, Canopy will be compatible.
